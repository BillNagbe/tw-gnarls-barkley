CeeLo Green

CeeLo Green, spent years with the rap duo Goodie Mob, and associating acts like Outkast, and Dungeon Family. Green, born Thomas Decarlo Callaway-Burton. In Atlanta, Georgia. Green started his career in the early ninties, he released three albums with the group Goodie Mob, before leaving to purse a solo career. He would release two solo albums that did okay, but did not reach the commercial success he once had. After putting out two albums with Danger Mouse(Gnarls Barkley), he would continue his solo career and see his greatest commercial success with his third album The Lady Killer. His most success single would be "Fuck You", released in 2010. The song would peak at two on the billboard Hot 100, Green has since had a success tv career as a judge on the hit tv show the Voice.
